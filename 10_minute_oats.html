    10-Minute Apple and Peanut Butter Oatmeal

    Serves one broke, hungry college student

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 cup old fashioned oats
    • 1/2 cup almond milk
    • 1 apple
    • 1 tsp. maple syrup
    • 1 tsp. cinnamon
    • A pinch of salt
    • 1 tbsp peanut butter

    Instructions

    1. Toast dry oats in a small pot over medium heat.
    2. Blend for one minute, or until smooth
    3. Add almond milk and blend again for a few seconds for a smoother texture
    4. Add chopped cashews to top
