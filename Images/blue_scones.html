    • Vegan Berry Scones

    Might as well live up to my username. It's only a matter of time before someone who calls herself "Leavemeascone" actually makes scones. Luckily, the first time was pretty successful.

    Scones seem like the kind of recipe that's super complex and easy to mess up, but in reality, I've never made a scone that completely failed. This recipe required a lot of kneading, and since I decided to put the berries in a little too early, the dough ended up turning blue.

    Is this a problem? Nah, they're just blue scones.

    Vegan Berry Scones

    Makes 16 scones

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups all purpose flour
    • 2 tsp. baking soda
    • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
    • 1/2 tsp. salt
    • 1/3 cup sugar
    • 1/2 cup non-dairy butter
    • 1/2 cup or fresh or frozen berries
    • 1/2 cup almond milk
    • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
    • 2 tbsp. almond milk

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
    2. Prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper
    3. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt together in a bowl
    4. Add butter to flour mixture, and use two knives or forks to break apart well
    5. Mix in sugar, butter, milk and berries
    6. Flour a clean surface and work the dough into a large circle, about 1/2 inch thick
    7. Use a pizza cutter to slice the dough into triangles. I made 16 scones, but you could do whatever you want ;)
    8. Place scones on your baking sheet and bake for 10-13 minutes, or until scones are a little toasted on top
    9. Let the scones cool for at least 20 minutes.
    10. In the meantime, mix together your powdered sugar and extra almond milk in a small bowl to make a glaze .Once the scones are cool, spoon the glaze on top
