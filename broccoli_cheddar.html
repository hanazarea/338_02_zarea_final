    • Vegan Broccoli Cheddar Soup

    Nutritional yeast is life ya'll. Even if you're not vegan, this stuff is life-changing. Not only does it taste like cheese, but it's pretty low calorie and has a bunch of nutritional benefits (hence the name, right?).

    Soup is also life, so that's why I'm sharing with you this awesome recipe that I tried last month. I like to eat it as a side dish or with a big scoop of brown rice. Enjoy!

    Vegan Broccoli Cheddar Soup

    Serves 4-6 hungry college students

    Ingredients

    • 1 pound of potatoes
    • 1 small/medium head of broccoli
    • 1 cup nutritional yeast
    • 1/2 tsp onion power
    • 1 tsp. garlic powder
    • 1/2 tsp. cayenne powder
    • 1 container of vegetable stock
    • Salt (to taste)

    Instructions

    1. Cube potatoes into 1 inch pieces and place into a large pot with spices, nutritional yeast and stock.
    2. Place on stovetop and bring to a boil
    3. Lower heat and continue to cook for 10 minutes, or until potatoes are soft
    4. Add in broccoli and cook for another 10 minutes
    5. Remove pot from heat
    6. Use an immersion blender to blend all of the ingredients, so that there are no more lumps left in the soup
    7. Add more salt or seasoning to taste, if you'd like
